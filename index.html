Some interesting facts about Camels are:

• Camel's ears are furry.

• Camels have two rows of thick eyelashes to protect their eyes from the desert dust.

Some interesting facts about Chameleons are:

• Chameleons can't regrow their tails.

• Their feet work like Salad Tongs.

• Skin Crystals enable them to change color at will.

Some interesting facts about Cicadas are:

• Most Cicadas have red- orange eyes.

• They have one of the longest insect life spans.

• They can reach three inches in lengh.

Some interesting facts about Pandas are:

• Pandas have 6 toes to grasp bamboo.

• Pandas like to lick copper and iron.

• An Adult Panda can eat 12-38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Some interesting facts about Pigeons are:

• Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.

• They understand space and time.

• One study suggests that, given the right conditions, they're as good at identifying cancer as doctors
